Behind the Scenes of Strange Harvest

Behind the scenes of Strange Harvest, the process resembled building an investigative archive more than producing a conventional feature. Interviews, archival broadcasts, reenactments, and investigative threads were developed as standalone pieces and refined over multiple years before final assembly.

The production relied on a compact team focused on realism, continuity, and worldbuilding detail. The result is a fully fabricated case file presented with documentary precision across performance, locations, props, and visual storytelling.
